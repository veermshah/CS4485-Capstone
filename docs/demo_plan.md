#Demo Plan

1) Open the dashboard and show the general map view.
2) Toggle pre-disaster vs post-disaster imagery layers.
3) Show building polygons/annotations on the map.
4) Click one building to see pre/post image crops and a predicted damage label (demo output).

- Inputs: pre/post imagery + building polygons/labels
